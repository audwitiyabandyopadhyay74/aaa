What are functions? 

 

  • Suppose you decided to make a sandwich of peanut butter and jelly. What instruction list looks easier? :

 

  1. Pick up bread
  2. Get your knife
  3. Open the peanut butter
  4. Put on the knife with peanut butter
  5. Spread on bread with peanut butter
  6. And so on, and so on

 

  • Functions are a way to group these instructions.
  • Functions have a block of code that is executed only when the function is called.
  • A function may also return some value.
